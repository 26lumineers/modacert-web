# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

ModaCert serves collectors, resellers, buyers, and owners of luxury goods who need a clear authentication path before purchase, resale, or personal record keeping. They are often comparing a specific item under time pressure and need the interface to feel precise, trustworthy, and easy to complete.

## Product Purpose

ModaCert authenticates designer bags, fashion, sneakers, watches, jewelry, and accessories through expert review. Success means a user can identify the right brand and item category, upload the required proof, pay, and receive a trackable authentication order without uncertainty.

## Brand Personality

Refined, exacting, assured. The brand should feel like a luxury service desk with expert operators behind it: calm enough for trust, direct enough for checkout, and visually distinctive enough to belong near designer goods.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, cold fintech blues, playful marketplace styling, and template-heavy landing pages with repeated icon cards or overused section labels. The product should not feel like a casual resale app, an AI-generated luxury mockup, or a payment form pasted onto a marketing page.

## Design Principles

- Lead with tangible luxury goods and expert trust, not abstract claims.
- Keep every step visibly connected to the user's item and order.
- Use bold orange and deep black as deliberate ModaCert signals.
- Let the workflow feel guided without hiding the user's choices.
- Preserve visual polish while keeping form controls readable and operable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic form labels, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that work from the Figma iPhone frames through desktop. Decorative imagery should not replace required text or status information.
